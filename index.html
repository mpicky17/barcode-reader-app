Barcode Detection

FREE — No paid AI required

Uses ZXing-js, a free open-source library that decodes barcodes using traditional image processing — no AI model involved. Supports UPC-A, EAN-13, and all standard grocery barcode formats.

Paid AI alternatives (Google Cloud Vision, AWS Rekognition) can also read barcodes but cost money per call and offer no practical advantage over ZXing-js for standard retail barcodes.
